All pups will be sold with a Limited Registration* see below

*I prefer to sell to homes that do not intend to breed. I rarely will make an exception on an individual basis with people I know or who have a history on Entry Express AND are Approved. Breeding has become very complicated with EIC testing; therefore, all pups will be sold with a Limited Registration.

This means you can not breed them. You may have it changed to Full Registration once the dog is 2 years old and has successfully passed all its health certifications. The health certifications are OFA hips and elbows, Eye Certification done by a licensed Ophthalmologist, and DNA testing for EIC and CNM if needed. You must also pay me an additional $200. The dog must have attained a field title of at least SH. Since I sell on Limited Registration, I do not do EIC testing on puppies. If you want EIC testing on pups prior to purchase you are welcome to pay for the testing.

*With Limited registration you may still enter any AKC event except the conformation show ring: that means obedience, agility, hunt test etc. If the puppy would be CNM and EIC Clear, and I know you or you can demonstrate responsible breeding practices, I may issue a full registration after all the health certs are done (Hip OFA, & Elbows and Eye CERF) and $200 and a title of at least Senior hunter.
