Erins Edge Double Magnum Ben, SH
DOB: 6-19-2004, OFA#161370 Excellent, EL31698, CERF 41257, should not carry Y
Frozen semen breeding $600 + $125 (prior to semen shipment) , no Natural
CNM Clear CNM05-320, EIC Clear DO8-041169 PRA Clear 10-80834
Sperm collected and frozen 6/08 1.25 Billion, thaw rate 90%

Ben was retrieving ducks in the water at 2 1/2 months and walking with them at hold. He has great bone, is very dark, and as you can see getting more handsome every year and he also has a pleasing, happy personality. Ben has a lot of natural hunting ability with an outstanding nose, which some times gets him in trouble in competition, but he will not quit until he digs out the birds. He naturally stays close hunting, and is quiet as his sister in the blind. My Ben was 78-80# but is now is 72# (I keep him slim as he gets older to help him be an excellent and agile breeder). He is a square dog and looks like a Labrador should but with an all field background. Ben can breed and then gently play with a puppy afterwards, he loves kids, and is very extremely affectionate. He sires big litters and is the best choice for a stud for an all around family/hunting litter.

Ben is producing some very natural, birdy good looking retrievers just like him that make excellent hunters, HT dogs and family companions. See the 2008 News page. He is linebred on CFC/CAFC Pachanga Magnum Force. His grandmother is my QAA bitch from Pachanga. Ben's siblings are very natural hunters. His father GRHRCH Gator Pt's Magnum Gentle Ben MH,QAA held 3 titles, passed 72 straight hunt tests, and was rated the #3 sire in HRC/UKC history when he was alive. Gentle Ben's full sister is the only FC/AFC chocolate female in AKC history and has produced many field champions. My Ben has sired Master hunters & Senior Hunters.
