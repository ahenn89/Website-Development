Products


Since Eldora Marshall opened the Natural Food Shop in 1960, we have been committed to providing products that are good for your body. Simple, honestly natural, personal care products that are safe for adults, children, pets, and the Earth


We specialize in products that are hard to find or not stocked by other stores. We can provide a range of efficacious, health and fitness products that fit any budget.


We choose products from manufacturers who are scrupulous about the integrity of their ingredients and production process, and use cruelty free testing procedures. Consult with our staff to decide which product is best for you.


Can't find what you want on our shelves? Often times we can custom order a product for you. Just ask and we will do our best to find the product you want.


We can help you find the right super-foods and supplements to achieve your health and fitness goals. Our experienced and trained staff can help you understand what products and practices can help you overcome ailments, build your health, strength and stamina, and maintain your optimum life functions.


The Natural Food Shop has the largest selection of individual, combination, and topical homeopathic remedies in the Milwaukee area. We also provide the full range of cell salts in either individual or combination components.


We stock a complete range of Essential oils. We also stock several models of aromatherapy diffusers.


Educate yourself about the properties and uses of essential oils. Consult a knowledgeable reference for indications and contraindications before use. Due to the powerful nature of steam distilled, pure essential oils we suggest you use these products with caution. We suggest that you consult your aromatherapist, alternative health care practitioner, or health professional when using essential oils or fragrances
